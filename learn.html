How to Use Signals

Set Time Zone to UTC -3:

  • Log in to your Quotex broker account.
  • Go to Settings or the Time Zone section.
  • Change your time zone to UTC -3 to match the signal timing.

Access the Signals:

  • Visit the Signals page on our website.Or By Login on our page
  • Look for the latest signal details, including:
    • Pair Name
    • Time
    • Signal Type
    • Time To
    • Gale

Trade Execution:

  • Use a 5-minute time frame for all trades.
  • Execute the trade based on the Signal Type (e.g., BUY/CALL or SELL/PUT).

Follow Gale Strategy:

  • If the first trade results in a loss:
    • Immediately place the same trade for the second Gale time mentioned in the signal.
    • Adjust your stake size as per your risk management plan for Gale trades.

Keep Refreshing the Signals Page:

  • The signals are updated regularly.
  • Refresh the page frequently to see the latest signals and act promptly.
Note: Always ensure your trade settings match the signal details to avoid errors.use the same time given in the signals page. Also may some trades fail but not all so keep watch.
